This design demonstrates the practical use of **Mask**, **Blending Modes**, and **Effects** in Figma to create a visually meaningful and layered composition.

### Mask

A human head silhouette was used as a **mask** to contain a landscape photograph. By placing the image inside the shape and applying *Use as Mask*, the photo is visible only within the head outline. This technique visually represents ideas, freedom, and imagination while showing how masking controls content visibility.

### Blending Modes

Blending modes were applied to merge the masked image smoothly with the background. Modes such as *Soft Light* or *Overlay* were used so that the edges of the silhouette blend naturally into the background instead of appearing cut out. This improves visual harmony and creates a more realistic depth effect.

### Effects

Effects were used to add depth and focus:

* Background Blur was applied to the outer area to separate the subject from the background.
* Shadows and glow were added around the head shape to make the masked area stand out and feel elevated.
